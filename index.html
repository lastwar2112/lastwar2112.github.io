Server Rules

guidelines
01

No plundering trucks or Secret Tasks

Please refrain from plundering other members' trucks or Secret Tasks. Violations will result in a 3-hit penalty.

02

Top 20 Total Hero Power Capitol Buff

Members ranked in the Top 20 by Total Hero Power will receive a buff priority. We encourage everyone to keep growing their heroes.

03

City Contest scheduling

City Contest can only be held on Wednesdays. If a contest is planned, please announce it at least 24 hours in advance. Last-minute notices are not permitted, so that everyone has a fair chance to prepare.
